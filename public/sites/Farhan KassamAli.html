Al-Ummah

Al-Ummah has been an amazing experience. I have gotten the opportunity to experience a plethora of awesome and cool things that have changed who I am as a person

Pros Cons
You meet people from all over the country The friendships established are truly one of a kind You gain a deeper understanding of what it means to be an Ismaili You get a rest hour, bhas, bais, uncles, and aunties that are just the best
The food is an average 4/10 You have 12 hour days No phone, no internet, no gum, no candy, and no soda

So should I consider applying?

All I can say is that I have loved every moment of being in Al-Ummah, through all the late nights and early mornings and terrible dinners to singing together. It is an experience of a lifetime that I would highly recommend for everyone. And if you don't trust me, trust Aly Uncle
